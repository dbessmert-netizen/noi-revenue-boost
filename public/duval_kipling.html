The model, in one line
Center Creek Capital Group buys the system from NOI and finances it over 25 years at 8.99%. Because Center Creek Capital Group owns it, the IRS returns the 30% ITC plus MACRS depreciation — applied to the loan, this cuts the real monthly cost roughly in half. Residents pay a little under their old utility bill, and Center Creek Capital Group keeps the spread as new NOI, growing up to 3% per year. Zero upfront capital, zero operational burden — NOI funds, installs, maintains, and bills.
